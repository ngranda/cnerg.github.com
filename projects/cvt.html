Nuclear Security and Non-Proliferation¶

As part of the Consortium for Verification Technology (CVT), Cyclus is being used to study issues in nuclear security and non-proliferation. Research areas include the incorporation of social-behavioral models to agent interactions, nuclear material tracking and forensics, and detector sensitivity estimation.

Cyclus sits at the intersection of several thrusts of the CVT mission. Cyclus incorporates signals available to inspectors with behavorial models for diversion to inform detector development and analysis techniques.
